Privacy Policy — Armor of God

Last updated: July 1, 2026

Armor of God ("the app") is a free, offline Bible activity for children based on Ephesians 6. Your privacy — and your child's — matters to us. This policy explains, in plain language, how the app handles information.

Information we collect

None. The app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. There are no accounts, no sign-in, and no forms. The app works entirely on your device and does not require an internet connection to play.

Analytics and tracking

The app contains no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking of any kind. We do not use cookies or identifiers to track you or your child across apps or websites.

Children's privacy

The app is designed for children and families. Because it collects no data whatsoever, it does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 (or any age). It is compliant with the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) by design.

Third-party services

The app does not integrate any third-party services, SDKs, or external content sources at runtime.

Changes to this policy

If this app changes in a way that affects privacy, this policy will be updated and the "Last updated" date above will change.
